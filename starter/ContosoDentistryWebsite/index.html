About Contoso Dental Care

We are proud to invite you to our Dental Practice located in Fairfax, directly across from the Fair Oaks Mall. Dr. Karma, as her patients affectionately call her, started her own practice in Fairfax with the goal of providing quality dental care to her patients. Her emphasis on patient comfort as well as keeping up-to-date with the latest advancements in dentistry enable her to provide her patients with the best quality of care in the field.

Dr. Karma holds Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree from Virginia Commonwealth University.  She works with pediatric, adult and geriatric patients.  Beyond general restorative procedures, she is experienced in periodontal treatment of gum, root canal treatments, surgical and non-surgical extractions, crowns and bridges, and restoration of implant supported crowns.
